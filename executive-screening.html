Executive Function & Attention Screening

This screening module evaluates attentional regulation and executive control patterns commonly associated with adult ADHD presentations. It is structured according to DSM-5 domains of inattention and hyperactivity-impulsivity, and modeled on validated adult self-report frameworks. This tool is research-informed and not a clinical diagnosis.
